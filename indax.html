Improved Feedback
Experience

Clients can record feedback in a format that is way
better than an email. Better feedback leads to an
improved collaboration experience and minimizes
needed revisions.

Next-level Proofing Tools

When you send work, clients can provide text-based feedback (just like an
email), or a screen recording of their feedback. Voice and annotation recording
provides a much richer feedback experience that is easier to give and much
more informative to receive. Try our feedback tools for an enriching and time-
saving collaborative experience.

Recorded Voice

Hearing pauses, tone of voice, hesitation,
excitement, and delight provides way more
information than in a wall of text. Relieve
clients from trying to figure out how to
express their feedback in words through an
offer a more natural feedback approach.

Annotations

Clients can draw annotations over work,
but unlike other tools that only give a static
image of the final annotation, you can see
the annotations being drawn while the
client narrates, making it feel like a live
feedback session.
